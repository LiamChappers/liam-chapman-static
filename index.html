What have I been up to?

2024

Finishing my travels

Returned to the UK after a short journey through USA to finish off my travels. I have resumed my love for front-end development and design.

I have kickstarted this by designing and developing this website as well as beginning work for new and existing clients. I can’t wait to see what the future holds as move on with the next step in my career as a front-end developer and designer!

2023

Canadian Road Trip

2023 saw the main bulk of my break from web development. I bought a van and travelled around Canada, visiting the rocky mountains, temperate rainforests and the badlands of alberta. We even managed to reach Alaska.

These travels inspired me to design and develop with nature in mind. I have moved my websites and email accounts to more sustainable servers and will always vow to work with the most sustainable methods possible as a software and website designer and developer!

2022

End of an Era, Start of a Journey

Having worked from home throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, I decided it was time to take a break from the screen and get exploring. In May 2022 I hopped on a plane to Los Angeles, where i started travelling through the west coast of USA before heading to Canada.

I went to several major cities in the states and stopped at many of the beautiful national parks on the way. I learnt some new skills such as snowboarding, and this is where my love for the outdoors truly developed.
